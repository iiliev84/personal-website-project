Soccer


Soccer is my passion and my hobby. In my free time I go with friends to play soccer or to watch soccer. Also I like to attend soccer games. I used to play for a team, but got injured and now I play for fun. My favourite soccer teams are Levski Sofia (Bulgaria), Real Madrid (Spain), Chelsea (England) and Inter (Italy). Cristiano Ronaldo is my favourite soccer player. Hristo Stoichkov is a famous bulgarian soccer player. He used to play for Barcelona in Spain and for Chicago Fire and D.C. United in USA.
